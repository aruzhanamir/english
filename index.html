Grammar

Past Simple, Past Continuous

Past Simple: 1. действия происходят один за другим 2. начались и закончились в прошлом
Past Continuos: 1. два действия. которые параллельно происходили в прошлом. 2. действие. указан срок в прошлом

Past habits

Past habits: used to, would

Phrasal Verbs

  • Keep up with = move at the same speed/cope with changes
  • Keep on = continue
  • Keep away = prevent access to, hold back
  • Keep off = stay away, prevent from stepping/climbing on sth

Module 4

Grammar

Will/Be going to

Will: 1. a prediction based on what we think/believe 2. certainty
Be going to: 1. a future plan 2. a prediction based on what we see

Present continuous (future meaning)

In present: an action happening now
In future: a fixed arrangement in the near future
